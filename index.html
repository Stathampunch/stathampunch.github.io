Time to Statham punch

How long do you have to wait?

About

A few years ago, I started tracking how long it took Jason Statham to punch someone (TTSP) in his films, and then I realized it should exist as a service to all, so this site was born. The first punch by Statham is any punch thrown in anger at another person during his films, ideally at the face. Fist bumps or friendly shoulder punches don't count. Time starts as soon as the movie begins.

The Data

I love Jason Statham's acting and movies (even his serious ones!) but he's in his element when he's punching someone and I love that almost every movie he makes has him doing just that at least once.

Suggestions

Want to help fill out the TTSP data for every film? Email the data to me and I'll add it to this site.
