About

After I tracked how long it took Jason Statham to punch someone (TTSP) in three recent films on Twitter, I realized it should exist as a service to all, and hence the TTSP site was born. The first punch by Statham is any punch thrown in anger during his films, ideally at the face. Fist bumps or friendly shoulder punches don't count.
