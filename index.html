About

Years ago, I started tracking how long it took Jason Statham to punch someone (TTSP) in his films, and then realized it should exist as a service to all. This site was born in 2015.

The first punch is any punch thrown in anger at another person — ideally at the face. Time starts as soon as the movie begins.

Suggestions

Want to help fill out the TTSP data for every film? Email the data and it'll be added to the site.
